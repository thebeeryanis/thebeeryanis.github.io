About the Band

Beeryanis isn't just a band—it's a vibe. Emerging from the Southern alternative scene, we're a collective of musicians who channel raw emotion, rebellious energy, and the kind of honesty that only comes from living life on your own terms.

Our sound fuses classic rock influences with modern edge, creating anthems for anyone who's ever felt like an outsider. With lyrics that hit hard and melodies that stick with you, Beeryanis is here to remind you that it's okay to be different, flawed, and unapologetically yourself.
