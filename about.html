About Beeryanis

Born from the ashes of failed relationships and garage band dreams, Beeryanis emerged as a force in the alternative rock scene.

Our Story

Beeryanis started as a therapeutic outlet for a group of musicians who found themselves navigating the complexities of divorce, fatherhood, and reinvention. What began as late-night jam sessions fueled by cheap beer and raw emotion evolved into something much more powerful.

Our sound captures the essence of divorced dadrock - honest, unfiltered, and unapologetically real. We write about the messy parts of life that most bands gloss over: the pain of separation, the joy of rediscovery, and the strength that comes from picking up the pieces.

Our Sound

We blend classic rock influences with modern alternative edge, creating anthems that resonate with anyone who's ever felt like an outsider. Our lyrics cut deep, our riffs hit hard, and our melodies stick with you long after the song ends.

Our Mission

Beeryanis exists to remind people that it's okay to be broken, flawed, and human. Through our music, we celebrate the resilience of the human spirit and the beauty that can emerge from life's most challenging moments.
